General Information

The 19th Workshop on Virtualization in High­-Performance Cloud Computing (VHPC 2024) aims to bring together researchers and industrial practitioners facing the challenges posed by virtualization in HPC/Cloud scenarios in order to foster discussion, collaboration, mutual exchange of knowledge and experience, enabling research to ultimately provide novel solutions for virtualized computing systems of tomorrow.

The workshop will be held in conjunction with the International European Conference on Parallel and Distributed Computing (Euro-Par 2024), on Aug 27th, 2024, in Madrid, Spain.

For more information, refer to either the VHPC 2024 or the Euro-Par 2024 web pages.
