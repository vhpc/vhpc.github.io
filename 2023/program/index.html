General Information

The 18th Workshop on Virtualization in High­-Performance Cloud Computing (VHPC 2023) aims to bring together researchers and industrial practitioners facing the challenges posed by virtualization in HPC/Cloud scenarios in order to foster discussion, collaboration, mutual exchange of knowledge and experience, enabling research to ultimately provide novel solutions for virtualized computing systems of tomorrow.

The workshop will have a duration of one day and it will be held in conjunction with the International Supercomputing Conference - High Performance (ISC) 2023 on May 25th, 2023 in Hamburg, Germany.

For more information, refer to either the VHPC 2023 or the ISC 2023 web pages.
